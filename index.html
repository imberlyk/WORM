Softness as a concept and practice has frequently been sidelined in mainstream discourse, often perceived as synonymous with fragility or a lack of resilience. This project seeks to question that history by exploring new ideas of ‘softness’ including its contemporary relevance and function. Through the act of gathering, this ongoing collection (authored by many), puts diverse ideas of ‘softness’ in proximity to one another in order to surface the many dimensions of how ‘softness’ shapes our socio-political and cultural landscapes in subtle yet significant ways.
